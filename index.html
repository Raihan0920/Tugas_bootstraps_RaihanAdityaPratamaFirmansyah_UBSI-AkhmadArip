WELCOME TO TUTORIALS HACKERS


Hackers are people whose programming skills are able to break through computer security systems or computer networks for certain purposes. A hacker has an advanced understanding of computers, networks, programming, or hardware.

Knowing what a hacker is can understand that hackers are not always synonymous with cybercriminals. In the cyber world, hackers are people who can hack devices such as computers, cell phones, webcams, to routers. Hacker actions that harm certain parties are criminal acts. However, in certain cases, hackers are figures who can also be profitable.

Generally, hackers are divided into three, there are black hat hackers, white hat hackers, gray hat hackers, and blue hat hackers.

  1. Blue hat is a type of hacker who hacks with the motive of revenge against a company or an organization. Usually blue hats are former employees who are upset at being laid off or dissatisfied with the treatment given by the company.
  2. If Black Hat uses hacking abilities to steal personal data and other criminal acts.
  3. White Hat is just the opposite. They try to hack into a system or program to find a security hole. White Hat also works according to regulations and through legal methods.
